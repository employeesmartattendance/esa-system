How It Works

Simple. Automated. Reliable.

From school setup to daily attendance — ESA makes the entire process effortless in four simple steps.

01
School Registration
Super admin creates your school profile on the platform, assigns a school admin account, and configures the GPS boundary and working hours.
02
Add Teachers
School admins register teachers with their profiles. Each teacher receives login credentials and can download the ESA mobile or desktop app.
03
GPS Check-In
Teachers open the app and tap check-in. ESA verifies their GPS location against the school boundary and records the exact timestamp automatically.
04
Live Dashboard
Admins see real-time attendance on a live map and dashboard. Reports are generated automatically, and auto-checkout handles end-of-day operations.
Platform Features

Everything Your School Needs

A complete attendance management platform built specifically for educational institutions.

GPS Location Verification
Attendance is verified using precise GPS coordinates. Teachers must be within the configured school boundary radius to successfully check in — no proxy attendance possible.
Wi-Fi Network Validation
Optional Wi-Fi BSSID-based verification adds an additional layer of security. Teachers must be on the school's Wi-Fi network to check in, ensuring true on-premise verification.
Real-Time Live Dashboard
Watch attendance happen live. The dashboard updates in real-time via WebSockets, showing who is present, late, or absent the moment a check-in is recorded.
Automated Reports
Generate daily, weekly, and monthly attendance reports with one click. Export to CSV for payroll integration, HR systems, or administrative review.
Multi-School Management
Super admins manage unlimited schools from a central control panel. Each school operates independently with its own settings, teachers, and attendance records.
Auto-Checkout System
Configure automatic end-of-day checkout times. Teachers who forget to check out are handled automatically, keeping records clean and accurate without manual intervention.
